Lunch Bunch
Works with existing saves, as well as multiplayer games (install on host)
Drop the folder into 7 Days to Die/Mods

Adds recipes for a couple lunch box items. Pack up some food for trips!

Adds recipe for hot/cold weather clothes.
Adds recipes to bundle up ammo (currently just base, not AP or HP)
Adds recipes for piggy bank items to bundle up your cash
